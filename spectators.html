Tournament Info

event

When

Monday, August 31 – Sunday, September 6, 2026. Pro and amateur play runs throughout the week, building to championship finals on the final weekend.

location_on

Where

Cary Tennis Park, 2727 Louis Stephens Drive, Cary, NC 27519 — a premier outdoor facility in the heart of the Triangle.

sports_tennis

What

A Carvana PPA Tour Slam featuring the world's best pros alongside amateur brackets for every age and skill level.

Daily Schedule

Detailed daily schedules, the pro field, and order of play will be published closer to the event. Follow us on social and check back for updates.

Parking & Directions

local_parking

On-Site & Lot Parking

Event parking details and any shuttle service will be confirmed before the tournament. Plan to arrive early on finals weekend.

local_taxi

Rideshare

A designated rideshare drop-off and pickup zone will be available near the main entrance for Uber and Lyft.

directions

Getting Here

Cary Tennis Park is roughly 20 minutes from Raleigh-Durham International Airport (RDU) and easily reached from I-40 and NC-540.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the event rain or shine?expand_more
Play continues in most conditions. In the event of severe weather, schedules may be adjusted for safety — updates will be shared on-site and via social media.
Is the venue accessible?expand_more
Yes. Cary Tennis Park offers accessible parking, entrances, and seating areas. Reach out via our contact page for specific accommodations.
Will there be food and drinks on-site?expand_more
Absolutely. The festival village features a wide range of food vendors, bars, and concessions throughout the grounds.
Can I bring my kids?expand_more
Pickleball is a family-friendly event! Youth admission policy will be confirmed closer to the tournament.
How do I become a volunteer?expand_more
Volunteer opportunities will open ahead of the event. Reach out through our contact page to be notified.
