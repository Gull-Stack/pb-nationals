Ticket Questions

Are tickets digital?expand_more
Yes. All tickets are mobile and delivered through Tixr. Add them to your phone's wallet ahead of time and have your screen ready at the gate.
Do children need a ticket?expand_more
Children's admission policy will be confirmed closer to the event. Check back or contact us for the latest youth ticketing details.
Can I upgrade or transfer my tickets?expand_more
Manage, transfer, and upgrade tickets directly in your Tixr account. Hospitality upgrades are subject to availability.
What's the difference between playing and watching?expand_more
Spectator tickets are sold on Tixr. If you want to compete as a pro or amateur, register to play through PickleballTournaments.com.
