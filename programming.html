More Than A Tournament

NC Programming 2026

A weeklong celebration of pickleball — live music, fan activations, on-court experiences, and one-of-a-kind theme nights. Here's everything happening at Cary Tennis Park.

Special Events On-Court Programming Stage Entertainment Theme Nights

All Week Long

Special Events

icecream Every Day

Midday Mixers

Complimentary afternoon socials with fun snacks and specialty treats onsite throughout the week — past offerings have included cotton candy, popcorn bars, dirty sodas, s'mores, and more. Open to participants and fans alike.

redeem Every Day

Weekly Raffle

Enter free raffles all week for a chance to win prizes from our incredible sponsors — paddles, Kitchen Blockers, VERTEX paddle cleaning blocks, Lock Laces, and more.

draw Every Day

Poster-Making Station

A complimentary fan activation where guests create custom posters and signs to cheer on their favorite pros and amateur players throughout the event.

mic Wed Night

Courtside Karaoke

Fans and players take Championship Court for a one-of-a-kind karaoke experience, singing live while lyrics light up the 360° LED boards.

local_bar Weekend

Pros Behind the Bar

PPA pros step behind the bar to serve drinks, take photos, and sign autographs in a fun, interactive meet-and-greet atmosphere.

Play Where The Pros Play

On-Court Programming

emoji_events Tuesday

King of the Court

A fast-paced, social round-robin where players rotate courts every 10 minutes based on results, working toward the "King Court." Compete against similar skill levels in an energetic atmosphere.

sports_tennis Wednesday

Play With the Pros

Join a PPA Tour professional on court for interactive gameplay, strategy development, skill-building, and live coaching alongside some of the sport's top athletes.

child_care Thursday

Free Youth Clinic

Kids learn the game, develop new skills, and interact with touring pros. Hosted in partnership with the Junior PPA program and designed for all experience levels.

school Tue / Thu

Pro Clinics & On-Court Experience

PPA pros such as Conner Garnett and Tyson McGuffin host instructional clinics — hands-on coaching, strategy instruction, and personalized interaction in an intimate setting.

nightlife Nighttime

Glow-in-the-Dark Pickleball

An "Exglowsive" experience — play in a neon-lit environment with glow elements, music, and high-energy nighttime gameplay.

Courtside Commons

Stage Entertainment

music_note Daily · 2–4PM

Serve & Sound Series

A live concert series with local North Carolina artists in the Courtside Commons — afternoon entertainment with drinks, music, and a fun social atmosphere.

draw_abstract After Matches

Player Autograph Sessions

Meet your favorite pros on stage after they finish their matches — photos and autographs during dedicated signing sessions all week.

quiz All Week

Trivia

Test your pickleball knowledge for fun and prizes between matches on the Courtside Commons stage.

forum All Week

Sponsor & Player Panels

Live talking panels with sponsors and players — insights, stories, and Q&A from across the world of pro pickleball.
